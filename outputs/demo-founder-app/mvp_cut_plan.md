# SpecForge MVP Cut Plan

## Recommended MVP Cut
- Anchor the first release on this primary goal: Help founders clean up rough product ideas
- Defer secondary goals until the first workflow is validated.
- Choose one primary platform instead of splitting across: local-first, web.
- Remove enterprise-only requirements such as SSO, audit trails, or compliance-heavy scope from MVP.
- Cut the release to one role and one core workflow before keeping the stated deadline.

## Why This Is Risky
- A small team, aggressive deadline, and broad scope are in tension.
- The brief calls this a simple MVP while also asking for enterprise breadth, many integrations, or multi-platform support.
- A one-person team plus an explicit delivery timeline increases schedule risk.
